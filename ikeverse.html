Kumulipo: The Creation Chant

Introduction

The Kumulipo is a sacred Hawaiian creation chant that traces the genealogy of the Hawaiian people and the cosmos itself.

3D Animation: The Birth of the Cosmos

The Chant

The chant begins in the deep darkness of Po (the night of creation):

"O ke au i kahuli wela ka honua
O ke au i kahuli lole ka lani
O ke au i kukaʻiaka ka lā..."

This describes the time when the earth became hot, the heavens turned, and the sun appeared in the sky.

Knowledge Check

What does Kumulipo mean in Hawaiian?

Correct! Kumulipo refers to the source of darkness, the primordial darkness from which all life emerges.
